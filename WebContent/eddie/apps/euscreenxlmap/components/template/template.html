Explore The EUscreen Collection

The EUscreen portal provides access to television clips and programmes from 24 broadcasters and audiovisual archives from 20 European countries. The EUscreen collection comprises thousands of media items and as well as videos it includes photographs, audio files and documents. All items have been hand-picked by experienced archivists and researchers for inclusion in this collection. Select a country to find out more about the EUscreen collection.

Unfortunately Explore The Euscreen Collection only works in tablet or desktop. Please use bigger devices to access this page.
