Explore The EUscreen Collection

The EUscreen portal provides access to television clips and programmes from broadcasters and broadcast archives from 20 European countries. The EUscreen collection contains nearly 60 000 media items, mostly videos, but it also includes photographs, audio files and documents. All items have been hand-picked by experienced archivists and researchers for inclusion in this collection. Select a country to find out more about the EUscreen collection.
